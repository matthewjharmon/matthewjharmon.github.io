About Me

As a technologist since the ’90s, I have a strong track history of building resilient infrastructure and teaching the next generation about technology. I teach offensive and defensive cyber security and UNIX-like systems including; Linux, FreeBSD, OpenBSD, and HardenedBSD. Additionally, from time to time, I publish about cyber security, give interviews to local news stations, and present my research in public forums.

Early on in my career, I was active as an international standards developer, functioning as a liaison from ANSI to ISO and the ITU.
